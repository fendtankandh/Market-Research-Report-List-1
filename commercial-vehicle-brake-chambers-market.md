Piston-type brake chambers utilize a piston mechanism to apply braking force, offering robust performance in heavy-duty applications. Diaphragm-type brake chambers feature a flexible diaphragm that produces braking force through air pressure, known for their compact design and reliability. Other types include specialized or less common brake systems that cater to specific vehicle requirements. Together, these types address the diverse needs of the commercial vehicle sector.</p></p>
