<p><p>The electric vehicle (EV) battery market comprises various types, each suited for different applications. NCM (Nickel Cobalt Manganese) and NCA (Nickel Cobalt Aluminum) batteries are known for high energy density and are commonly used in high-performance EVs. LFP (Lithium Iron Phosphate) batteries offer safety and longevity, ideal for budget-friendly models. LCO (Lithium Cobalt Oxide) batteries, while effective, are less common due to cost concerns. LMO (Lithium Manganese Oxide) batteries provide good thermal stability, catering to diverse EV needs, while "Others" encompasses emerging technologies.</p></p>
