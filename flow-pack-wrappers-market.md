<p><strong>The Flow Pack Wrappers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal</li><li>Vertical</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flow pack wrappers are categorized into horizontal and vertical markets based on the orientation of the packaging process. Horizontal flow wrappers operate with products entering from the side, making them suitable for flat or bulky items like snacks and baked goods. In contrast, vertical flow wrappers feed products from above, ideal for smaller or granular items like candies, seeds, or powders. Each type caters to specific product shapes and sizes, ensuring efficient packaging solutions tailored to diverse industry needs.</p></p>
