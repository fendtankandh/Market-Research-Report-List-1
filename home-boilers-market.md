<p><p>The home boilers market primarily comprises non-condensing and condensing boilers. Non-condensing boilers operate at lower efficiencies, releasing waste heat through flue gases, while condensing boilers utilize advanced technology to recover heat from exhaust gases, achieving higher efficiency and lower emissions. Condensing boilers are gaining popularity due to their environmental benefits and energy savings, whereas non-condensing models are often preferred for their lower initial costs. The choice between them depends on efficiency needs, budget, and installation requirements.</p></p>
