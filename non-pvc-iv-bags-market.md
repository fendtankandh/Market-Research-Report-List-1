<p><p>The Non-PVC IV Bags market is segmented into single-chambered and multi-chambered types. Single-chambered bags are designed for straightforward fluid delivery, containing a single solution or medication, ideal for immediate use. Multi-chambered bags, on the other hand, feature separate compartments for different solutions, allowing for the safe mixing of incompatible drugs until administration. This flexibility is crucial for complex therapies, making multi-chambered bags increasingly popular in healthcare settings, where efficiency and patient safety are top priorities.</p></p>
