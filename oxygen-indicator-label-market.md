<p><p>Oxygen indicator labels are essential tools used to monitor oxygen levels in packaging and storage environments. The market is divided into two primary types: reversible and non-reversible oxygen indicator labels. Reversible labels can change color in response to oxygen presence but revert to their original state once conditions improve, allowing for multiple assessments. In contrast, non-reversible labels provide a permanent indication of oxygen exposure, offering a one-time assessment that alerts users to oxygen levels without the possibility of reset.</p></p>
