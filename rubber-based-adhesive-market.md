<p><p>The rubber-based adhesive market is primarily divided into two types: natural rubber-based adhesives and synthetic rubber-based adhesives. Natural rubber-based adhesives, derived from latex, offer excellent elasticity and adhesion properties, making them suitable for applications like woodworking and packaging. In contrast, synthetic rubber-based adhesives, produced from petrochemical sources, provide enhanced durability, temperature resistance, and versatility in various industrial and consumer applications. Both types are essential for industries such as automotive, construction, and electronics, addressing diverse bonding needs.</p></p>
