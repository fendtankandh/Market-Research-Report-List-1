<p><p>The semiconducting glass market is divided into two main types: negative type and positive type. Negative type semiconducting glass exhibits a decrease in conductivity when exposed to light, making it suitable for applications like photoresistors. In contrast, positive type semiconducting glass shows increased conductivity under light exposure, making it ideal for photovoltaic applications. Both types play crucial roles in various industries, such as electronics and renewable energy, leveraging their unique electrical properties for innovative technologies.</p></p>
